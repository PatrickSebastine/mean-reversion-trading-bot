### Key Observations

1. Mean reversion significantly outperforms momentum in the tested market regime (ranging/sideways)
2. ADA/USDT is the best individual performer (+$77 in 7 days)
3. Mean reversion works best on mid-cap altcoins with regular oscillations
4. Momentum triggers rarely on 15m timeframe — consider 1h or 4h for trend-following strategies
5. ETH underperforms with both strategies — high market efficiency makes it harder to exploit

### Caveats

- **100% win rate is not sustainable.** The 7-day test period was favorable for mean reversion. Longer periods with trending markets will produce losses.
- **Small sample size.** 11 mean reversion trades across 7 days is not statistically significant. At minimum, 30+ days of data is needed for reliable conclusions.
- **Original backtest excluded fees and slippage.** The refactored engine now accounts for these. Expect net returns to be 10–20% lower after realistic costs.
- **No slippage on stop losses.** In volatile markets, stop losses can gap through the target price, resulting in larger-than-expected losses.
- **Market regime dependency.** Mean reversion profits when markets oscillate in a range. During strong trends, this strategy will generate repeated stop-outs.
